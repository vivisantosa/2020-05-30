I am Hakka Moi - the Chinese "Gypsy"

I came from a landless tribe, a perpentual guest in stranger land. I call my self the citizen of the world, yet I often feel I am not an eathtling. Born raised among my Indonesian rich cultural, between my dad's Chinese heritage and my mother Dutch education, and grew from my exerience of living in 5 different countries, makes me attracted to cultures.
A traveler, a lover of arts and culture, I am an artist at heart. I took pictures or paint of the world. I wrote poems. I cook and bake - I made beautiful alcoholic cakes, cocktail icecream, and healthy and delicious gnocchi. My dream is to have a small cake shop on WallStreet, NYC, NY.
